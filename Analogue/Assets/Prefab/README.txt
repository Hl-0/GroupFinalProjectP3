Dr. Ryuta Kawashima - Brain Age: Train Your Brain in Minutes a Day!

Ripped by applecuckoo for the Models Resource, 2024

This model was ripped with apicula (https://github.com/scurest/apicula) to extract the model and 3DSKit (https://github.com/Tyulis/3DSkit) to dump the filesystem.

Colour tiles were reused from the previous unrigged version of Dr. Kawashima uploaded to tMR by Mystie, thanks to them!
